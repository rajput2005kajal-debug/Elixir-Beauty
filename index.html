Our Story

Elixir Beauty Co. was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to create clean, effective beauty products that enhance natural beauty without harsh chemicals.

Our founder, Sophia Chen, started mixing small batches of skincare in her kitchen after struggling to find products that worked for her sensitive skin. Today, we're proud to be a trusted name in clean beauty, with products sold worldwide.
