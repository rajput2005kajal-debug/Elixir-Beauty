Our Journey of Pure Beauty

From a small home studio to a global clean-beauty revolution — crafted with love, science, and nature.

How It All Began

In 2015, our founder Sophia Chen began experimenting with natural ingredients in her kitchen after struggling with sensitive skin. Her passion for purity and transparency led to the creation of Elixir Beauty Co.

What started with small handmade batches of organic creams quickly transformed into a trusted global brand known for clean, cruelty-free products that actually work.

Every Elixir formula is a blend of science and nature — designed to celebrate your skin’s natural glow.

“Beauty should feel pure, powerful, and kind.”

Our Mission & Values

We believe in beauty that respects both people and the planet. Our philosophy is rooted in purity, sustainability, and compassion.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly packaging and ethically sourced ingredients to protect the earth.

Cruelty-Free
